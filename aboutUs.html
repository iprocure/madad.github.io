How it all started

Facing the problem first-hand

Our founder & CEO, Rashid Al-Derham, with his experience in a leading financial institution in Qatar, experienced and understood how traditional financing failed to support small businesses.

While our co-founder Suresh Nambiar, from his B2B tech-venture background in Qatar, saw first-hand the cash flow struggles MSMEs faced.

Together, they recognized that MSME sector is crucial to the Qatar's economic growth but lacked accessible financing solutions, and are determined to change that.

With our first addition to the team, Ravindra Kumar bringing tech startup expertise, we started turning our vision into a scalable solution, starting with invoice discounting, empowering Qatar's entrepreneurs to thrive and contribute to the country's economic future.
